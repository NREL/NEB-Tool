Welcome to JUSTIFI!

Use this tool to identify and estimate potential non-energy benefits (NEBs) that can be found while conducting the energy assessments.

Find Non-Energy Benefits

Use the setup wizard to work through the process of finding non-energy benefits. In the wizard you will create and provide details for a company and facility. Then you will add energy assessment details that will allow you to discover additional non-energy benefits.

JUSTIFI Portfolio

View the companies, facilities and assessments inside of your JUSTIFI application from the portfolio window.
