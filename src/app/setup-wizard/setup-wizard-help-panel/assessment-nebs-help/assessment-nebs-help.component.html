If NEBs are found that cannot be associated with a specific energy efficiency opportunity, then they should be added here.

This could be used for both non-related NEBs found in facility walkthroughs and NEBs requiring the implementation of multiple energy efficiency opportunities. e.g. Implementing several opportunities results in being able to turn off equipment or meet a secondary goal.

Non-Energy Benefits can be added to assessment with the " Add NEB" button.

Clicking this button will give you two options for adding a new NEB:

  1. " Add Custom NEB" will add a free form Non-Energy Benefit to the assessment where the user can name the NEB and enter a direct financial impact in $/yr.
  2. " Search NEBs Database" will open a modal with a list of all the Non-Energy Benefits that are currently in the tool.

Once a NEB is entered, "Key Performance Metric Impacts" can be added. These are impacts that the NEB has on performance metrics the company is tracking.

The " Search Key Performance Metrics" button will open a modal with a list of all performance metrics in the tool. If a metric is known to be typically associated with the NEB, it will be identified with an .

Details about the performance metric and the impact the associated NEB will have can then be entered on the screen.

A list of NEBs found to be associated with specific energy efficiency opportunities is provided at the bottom of the screen.
